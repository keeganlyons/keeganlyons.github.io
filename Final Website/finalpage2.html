Surfing has been in so many movies that both you and I love. A couple of my personal favorites are Surfs Up and Fast Times at Ridgemont High. Not only do these movies show the stereotypical surfer, but they also show the viewer the surf culture. Surfing is all about bringing people together and having a good time. Surfs Up was able to get this across by bringing all the characters together at the end of the movie.

In Fast Times, Jeff Spicoli said, "Surfing is not a sport, its a way of life, you know a hobby. Its a way of looking at that wave and saying, 'Hey bud, lets party!'" Although some look at Jeff Spicoli as a punk surfer, at times he speaks words of wisdom. Surfing is a way of life.
